# Sira Path

## Short Description

Learn Islam step by step with playful paths, Quran and Sunnah lessons, and saved progress.

## Full Description

Sira Path helps learners grow in Islam through a colorful path of lessons rooted in the Quran and authentic Sunnah.

Build your foundation, strengthen your salah and wudu, explore the lives of the Prophets, learn the stories of the companions, study Quran by surah, and keep growing with review, streaks, mastery, and deeper branches over time.

Inside Sira Path you can:

- learn through a game-like lesson path
- build your basics in salah, wudu, iman, manners, fasting, zakat, and hajj
- explore Quran lessons by surah with tafsir and reflection
- study the lives of the Prophets and the companions
- save progress with an account
- keep up a learning streak
- review weak areas and return to lessons that need attention

Sira Path is designed to feel warm, motivating, and respectful while still making Islamic learning fun to return to every day.
