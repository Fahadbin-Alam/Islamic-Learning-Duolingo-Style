# Privacy Policy Draft

Sira Path respects your privacy and only collects the information needed to run the learning experience, save progress, and support accounts.

## Information We Collect

- account information such as name, email address, and sign-in provider
- lesson progress, XP, streaks, mastery, and review history
- question attempts and learning activity
- subscription and entitlement state when premium features are enabled
- optional social connection data that you choose to add

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- create and secure your account
- save your learning progress across sessions
- personalize lessons, review, and recommendations
- support streaks, reminders, and premium access
- improve the product and monitor reliability

## Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with service providers that help run authentication, hosting, analytics, notifications, or billing.

## Your Choices

You can:

- use core learning without creating an account where supported
- update account information
- log out of the app
- request account deletion when production account management is finalized

## Security

We use reasonable measures to protect account and progress data, but no system can guarantee absolute security.

## Children

Parents and guardians may use connected accountability features where available. Children should use the app with supervision appropriate to their age and local laws.
